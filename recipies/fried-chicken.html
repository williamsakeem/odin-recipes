Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

A quick, crispy chicken comes together with just chicken pieces, salt, black pepper, flour, and hot oil: season the chicken well, coat it lightly in flour, heat the oil until it’s shimmering, fry the pieces on medium heat until they’re golden and cooked through, then let them rest on a rack or paper towel so they stay perfectly crunchy.

Ingredients

    Chicken pieces (thighs or drumsticks)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Flour
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Season the chicken generously with salt and black pepper.
  2. Coat each piece evenly in flour, shaking off excess.
  3. Heat oil in a pan until hot enough for frying.
  4. Fry the chicken on medium heat until golden and fully cooked.
  5. Drain on a rack or paper towel to keep it crispy.
